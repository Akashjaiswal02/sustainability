New Features with Descriptions:

-Personalized Action Plan

-Generates a weekly eco-action plan customized to each user’s carbon footprint, location, and lifestyle.

-Suggests achievable tasks (e.g., “bike to work twice this week”) to help users steadily reduce emissions.

Green Rewards & Badges:

-Introduces a gamified point system where users earn “Eco Points” for completing sustainable actions.

-Points unlock digital badges, ranks, or discounts from eco-friendly brands.

Community Leaderboard:

-Displays top-performing users or local communities based on CO₂ savings and completed eco-actions.

-Encourages friendly competition and collaboration within the sustainability community.

Sustainability Tracker Dashboard:

-A visual analytics dashboard showing trends in the user’s carbon footprint reduction over time.

-Includes charts for energy, transport, waste, and water usage impact.

Eco Product Recommendations:

-Suggests verified sustainable products or local eco-businesses based on the user’s habits.

-Includes energy-efficient appliances, organic food markets, and refill stations.

Carbon Offset Integration:

Lets users directly invest in certified carbon offset projects (like tree planting or renewable energy).

Displays real-time progress of their offset contributions and global impact.
